rec.games.video.arcade.

mistakes can and probably do exist. I also am missing a couple of key 
pieces of info, like e-mail addresses and some names. Please send any 
additions, corrections, or comments to me at damone@ios.com. All are welcome.

-FAQS-
 
Format:
SUBJECT: Category of faq: Character, General, Miscellaneous, or Movelist.
TITLE: Title of faq.
CURRENT VERSION: The most recent version available.
AUTHOR: Author of faq: where comments should be directed.
CURRENT AVAILABILITY: Where the faq can be found. If 'Through author' is 
not listed, do not write him for the faq: use the other sources. 
'rec.games.video.arcade' means that the faq is posted on that newsgroup 
as updates warrant. 'FTP' is self-explanatory.
FTP SITES: Sites where the faq can be retrieved.
