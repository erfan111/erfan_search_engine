  This format of the QFAQ will print out on one page on a standard dot-matrix 
 printer.  It will print out in a smaller pitch and in two columns in order  
 to fit on a page.  Save this to a file, and print it out from the dos prompt   
 or from the editor.  It is probablly the easiest format to take with you to  
 your arcade.

 guide (vf-25.txt).  You can obtain it on net news (posted every friday), by  
 anonymous ftp from netcom.com under pub/vi/vidgames/faqs (thanks to Andy Eddy  
 of GamePro mag), or by fingering the e-mail address above.
